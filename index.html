The Venue

Two Buildings.
One Unforgettable
Experience.

Parallel Party Bar is unlike any venue in the greater Ashburn area. Anchored in Broadlands Center, our space features retractable garage-door facades that open seamlessly between our Party Bar and its award-winning sister venue, Parallel Wine & Whiskey Bar — creating a rare indoor-outdoor flow that transforms with your event.

Host an intimate cocktail hour indoors, then roll open the doors to the courtyard as the evening comes alive. With nearly 300 combined seats across both buildings when fully open, we scale effortlessly from a private 10-person executive lunch to a 140-guest wedding reception.

And when your guests need a drink, they're in the best hands in Northern Virginia. Our sister bar has been voted Best Wine Bar five consecutive years — and that same standard of excellence carries through to every event we host.
